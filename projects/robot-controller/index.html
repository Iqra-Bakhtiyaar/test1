Robot controller

Mar 2015- Apr 2015

As part of final project of my 1st year in Computer Science I developed software of a robot which could be controlled from a website. The main idea was to control this robot from anywhere around the world. This robot shown in the image could do simple movements such as moving forward, backwards, right, left and in circles.

Following is the website developed in Dr.Racket. This robot was also equipped with a camera and the output could be seen on the website.
